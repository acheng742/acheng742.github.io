UF AAA Website & Branding

My Role

Designer/Developer redesigning the website and refining visual brand strategies.

Project Goal

To make a connection with the user through presentation and to highlight unity among the UF Association of Asian Alumni, ultimately leading to contributions to help fund scholarships and educational resources.

Target Audience

College seniors nearing graduation and alumni.

Challenge

The previous website design did not have engaging designs that captured the visitor's experience. A complete redesign was needed to focus on the driving visitors to donate, participate in regional gatherings, and share their experiences through podcast interviews.

Results

Website traffic and engagment dramatically increased, 17 alumni particiated in podcast interviews, and over $20,000 was donated to the association within a year of launch.
