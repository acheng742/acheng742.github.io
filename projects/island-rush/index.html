Island Rush Food Delivery App

Island Rush is a regional food delivery app located in Honolulu, Hawaii. Island Rush strives to deliver food in a timely manner and offers a wide spectrum of restaurants to order from.

My Role

UX Designer designing an app for Island Rush from conception to delivery.

Project Goal

Design an app for Island Rush that allows users to easily order fresh dishes to be delivered.

Target Audience

Stay-at-home people and workers who lack the time or ability to prepare a meal.

Challenge

Busy workers and home-bound people lack the time or ability to prepare a meal. There are corporate level competitors that have dedicated mobile ordering apps that are successful. The goal is to create a product that was made by locals to compete in the market, improve sales, and have high customer satisfaction.

Research Study Details

I conducted interviews and created personas to understand the users I’m designing for and their needs. A primary user group identified through research was working adults who don’t have time to cook meals. This user group confirmed initial assumptions about Island Rush’s customers, but research also revealed that time was not the only factor limiting users from cooking at home. Other user problems included obligations, interests, or challenges that make it difficult to get groceries for cooking or go to restaurants in-person.

Findings

- People who are homebound don’t have the ability to prepare a meal

- Working adults don’t have time or energy to spend time cooking a meal

- Platforms for ordering food are not equipped with assistive technologies

- Unorganized menus in apps are often difficult to navigate through and order from

Initial Design Concepts

Usability Testing

KPIs

Time on task: How long does it take for a user to complete their order?

Drop-off rate: How often do users not complete and leave their order?

Conversion rate: How many users successfully places an order?

Tasks

- Ask user to select a restaurant of their interest to order from

- Ask user to Start ordering items from the restaurant they selected

- Ask user to add a customization to the order

- Ask user to confirm their order and complete the checkout process

Results

- Users need a way to select restaurants to ordering from without much thought

- Users need an option to customize their orders

- Users need a way to store their payment information​
