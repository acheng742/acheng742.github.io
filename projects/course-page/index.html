Course Page Design

My Role

UX Designer/Developer designing and developing designing a logged-in member course viewing page experience from conception to delivery.

Project Goal

The objective of this project was to refresh the look and feel of the page where users watch videos of the course they are studying.

Target Audience

Users who have a personal subscription or users who have a company-paid subscription.

Challenge

Because the main purpose of the page is to watch videos, the challenge was how to keep important information such as the course name, description, transcript, practice exam, and virtual lab offerings readily available, but not obstruent.

Research Study Details

A group of users who have been long-time members were interviewed about features they would find beneficial in their training process. It was determined that the video player should be the main focus of the page, while secondary information such as the description and episodes were placed in a convenient side panel for easy access. Functionality, usability, and experience were carefully considered.

Results

A few months after launch, users were asked to take a short survey about their experience. They provided positive feedback and annual retention rate for both B2C/B2B users were at 94%.
