Website Redesign

This project's goal was to make a connection with the user through the brand and presentation of the product offering, while strategically designing the UI to optimize conversions. There are two primary types of visitors: An individual consumer looking to start or grow their career, and an IT team leader looking to get their team up-to-speed or stay up-to-speed in the ever changing technical world. Visuals presentations of the brand and product were used to help the visitor absorb the competitive offering in minimal time.
